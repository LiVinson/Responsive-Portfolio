About Me

I am a native Floridian, born and raised in Orlando. After graduating from the University of Florida, I briefly moved to Baton Rouge, LA where I taught high school math before relocating to Charlotte, NC. While there, I worked in HR as a recruiting coordinator and client delivery manager. I recently relocated back to Orlando to spend time with family.

In my free time, I enjoy my fair share of both outdoor and indoor activities. I love to hike, kayak, and take road trips to any place with delicious food (so far, St. Louis has the best barbecue, in my opinion). I also enjoy flag football, where I usually play receiver. I also enjoy weekend binge watching sessions and I am currently making my way through a list of some of the top anime shows.
